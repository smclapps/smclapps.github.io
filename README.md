About Diagnosis Queen:
Do you have what it takes to become the Diagnosis Queen?

The Challenge: Match classic symptoms and clinical findings with the correct medical conditions. What do you do with a "unilaterally swollen leg"? That's right – deep vein thrombosis! But watch out: the clock is ticking, and the cases are getting trickier.

Your Career Ladder: Start in your pre-clinical years and work your way up! Through clinical rotations and your internship, your path leads all the way to Chief of Medicine. Will you achieve the ultimate status of Diagnosis Queen?

Your Equipment: Stethoscope: Highlights the first tile of a matching pair. Lollipop: Instantly solves the next pair. Coffee: Grants you 10 extra seconds of time.

Features: Playful Learning: Perfect for medical students, nurses, and amateur diagnosticians. Game Center Integration: Compete with friends and colleagues worldwide. Beautiful Design: Lovingly crafted characters and smooth gameplay.

Are you ready for your first shift? Download Diagnosis Queen now and kickstart your career!
